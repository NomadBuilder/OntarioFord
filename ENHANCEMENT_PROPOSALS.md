### 2. **Cost Comparison Section** (NEW)
**Purpose**: Show the true cost of privatization (Americanization)

**Key Stats from CCPA Report**:
- Private agency staff cost **3x more** than regular employees
- Private agencies account for **0.4% of hours** but **6% of costs** ($725M in 2022-23)
- In North West region: **17% of staffing costs** go to private agencies

**Visual**: Side-by-side comparison
- "What $725M could fund instead"
- "3x the cost for the same work"
- Regional breakdown showing which areas are most affected

**Impact**: Makes the waste tangible

---

### 3. **Hospital Crisis Context** (NEW)
**Purpose**: Show the consequences of underfunding

**Key Stats**:
- **66 of 134 hospitals** (49%) had deficits in 2023-24
- **63% of small hospitals** (<$100M revenue) in deficit
- Ontario ranked **33/38 OECD countries** in hospital beds per capita
- **Lowest per capita hospital spending** in Canada ($1,805 vs BC $1,902, Quebec $2,028)

**Visual**: 
- Map of Ontario showing hospitals in deficit
- Comparison chart: Ontario vs other provinces/countries
- "Hallway medicine is the norm" - with data

**Impact**: Shows the human cost, not just numbers

### 8. **The Vicious Cycle Diagram** (NEW)
**Purpose**: Explain how privatization (Americanization) creates its own demand

**Cycle**:
1. Government underfunds hospitals
2. Hospitals can't retain staff (Bill 124 wage suppression)
3. Staff leave for better pay/conditions
4. Hospitals forced to use expensive private agencies
5. Agency costs consume budget
6. Less money for public staff
7. More staff leave
8. **Repeat**

**Visual**: Animated circular diagram that users can step through

**Impact**: Shows this is systemic, not accidental

---

**Purpose**: Show Ontario's hospital system is failing

**Key Stats**:

- **Hallway medicine is the norm**
- Real per capita hospital spending: **4% increase 2013-2022** (most in 2022 with federal pandemic funds)
- **Seven years of real per capita cuts** over the decade

**Visual**: 
- Comparison charts: Ontario vs other provinces, vs OECD
- "This is what underfunding looks like"

**Impact**: Puts Ontario's crisis in global context
